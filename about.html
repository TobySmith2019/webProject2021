About

Welcome to Cucullo, your number one source for all things shirt and hoodie related. We're dedicated to giving you the very best products, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2004 by George Smith, Cucullo has come a long way from its beginnings in a classroom in Southland, New Zealand. When George first started out, his passion for helping other parents be more eco-friendly, providing the best equipment for his fellow societal members, drove him to action, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world at very low prices, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly, fair trade wing of the fashion industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
